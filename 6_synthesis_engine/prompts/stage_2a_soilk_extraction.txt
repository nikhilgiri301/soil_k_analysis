SOIL K PARAMETER DEFINITIONS:
- Soil K Supply: Potassium released from soil reserves and made available for processes
- Exchangeable K: Potassium readily available in soil solution and on exchange sites
- Non-exchangeable K: Potassium in clay minerals and organic matter requiring time for release
- Total Soil K: Complete soil K inventory including all chemical forms and pools
- K Balance: Net soil K change from all inputs minus all outputs over time
- K Release: Transfer of K from less available to more available soil pools
- K Cycling: Movement of K through soil-plant-atmosphere systems
